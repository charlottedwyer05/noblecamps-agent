Handover instructions

To transfer this agent to a NobleCamps company account:
1. Open Google Cloud Console → delete the current service account key
2. Create a new project under the company Google account
3. Repeat setup (enable Sheets API, create service account, download JSON)
4. Share the Google Sheet with the new service account email
5. Update credentials in the Settings tab above
